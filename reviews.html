A friend referred me to cats right meow when my older cat passed away. The lady that ran this cattery was wonderful. She helped me find the perfect kitten that would match my lifestyle and fill the space in my heart. I have now had Snowball for three years. He is a healthy, beautiful Himalayan Persian and my best friend. Thank you!

I bought my Bengal cat, Spotsy, from the Cats Right Meow cattery when he was only eight weeks old. This past week he came in 2nd place at the CFA cat fanciers association annual Las Angeles, California cat show. The Cats Right Meow breeders are well known for having excellent show cats. This is True! My Spotsy is only one year old and has already placed in shows. He is a beautiful well mannered cat with excellent bloodlines!

We recently bought our baby, Lilly. We were looking for a hypoallergenic pet since I have some slight allergies. The owner of Cats Right Meow was super helpful and assured us that if we needed to bring Lilly back, we could. So far, it has been a few months, and I have had no reactions to her; in fact, she has become the light of our life. She is energetic and even loves walking on a leash around the neighborhood. Thank you to the owner who helped us find our furrever baby at Cats Right Meow.

Our Abyssinian cat Leo is the best cat in the world. We bought him for our daughter. However, he has won over the hearts of the whole family. Thank you, Cats Right Meow!

Five years ago, we bought Victoria from the Cats Right Meow cattery. She was one of the most beautiful Himalayan cats we had ever seen. We knew immediately when we walked into the large cat room that she was the one for us. She ran to us and purred loudly. We took Victoria home that day and she became our fur baby. She is sweet and loves to cuddle. Recently we decided to expand our little family and get another kitten. The only place we will ever go is Cats Right Meow Cattery. So this time, we picked a rambunctious little lady and named her Isabella. Isie is undoubtedly a handful; however, it brings the kitten out in Victoria again. Isie will pounce on her, and Victoria will chase her around the house. We are so thankful for our to babies!
