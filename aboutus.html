Cats Right Meow is a local cattery offering three breeds of high-quality exotic cats. Our cats are provided the utmost care and have extensive indoor and outdoor play areas. All the parents are show quality cats located on-site, regularly vet checked, and a part of our family. Our kittens are socialized and played with all day, making the best family additions. We emphasize well-planned and intensively managed housing systems, husbandry, and selected animal breeding to provide the best-pedigreed lines of cats. We started with our love of cats and showing them off at TICA international cat shows before we moved into sharing that love with others. We hope you also consider opening your hearts and homes to some of that love.
